Origins

I found this shirt secondhand. I initially liked the look of the curved, appliqué letters. Then, I started to wonder what I could make the shirt say if I were to rip some of the letters off and rearrange them. I bought the shirt, went home, got out my seam ripper, and started tearing it apart.

I knew as soon as I thought of this combination, this is what I wanted it to say: UGH BORING.

Final Product

Over the next couple months, I digitally designed distressed and rearranged logos for a couple more universities. Here is the final product of UGH BORING, made from raw fabric, completely custom.
